Andrés Occhipinti Liberman

I'm an External Lecturer at the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computer Science, Technical University of Denmark (DTU).

I obtained my PhD degree in computer science (artificial intelligence and logic) from the same department, in November 2020.

Earlier, I completed the MSc in Logic at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation (ILLC), University of Amsterdam.

Keywords: artificial intelligence, multi-agent systems, planning, theory of mind, dynamic epistemic logic, learning symbolic representations.
