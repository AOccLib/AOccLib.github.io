Andrés Occhipinti Liberman

I'm a postdoctoral researcher within the Representation Learning for Planning (RLeap) project in the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Group of Universitat Pompeu Fabra.

Previously, I was an external lecturer at the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computer Science of the Technical University of Denmark (DTU). I received my Ph.D. degree in artificial intelligence and logic from the same department in November 2020.

Before that, I completed the M.Sc. in Logic at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation (ILLC) of the University of Amsterdam.

Keywords: artificial intelligence, social intelligence, multi-agent systems, dynamic epistemic logic, planning, learning symbolic representations.
